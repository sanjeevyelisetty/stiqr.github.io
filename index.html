How It Works

FOUR SIMPLE STEPS

01
📱

Register with your phone

Sign up using your mobile number. A one-time OTP is sent via SMS to verify your identity — secure and instant.

02
🏷️

Get your STIQR sticker

A unique QR code is generated and assigned to your account. Download and print your sticker in Vertical, Round, or Strip format.

03
📷

Finder scans the QR

If someone finds your lost item, they scan the STIQR code with any smartphone camera — no app download required to scan.

04
🔒

Connect via masked call

The finder reaches you through our Twilio toll-free relay. Neither party's real number is ever revealed to the other.

Privacy & SMS Use

YOUR PRIVACY IS OUR PRODUCT

Data & Privacy Policy

STIQR is built around the belief that recovering a lost item should never require giving a stranger your personal information. We collect only the minimum data needed to operate the service.

  • Your mobile number is stored securely to power your account identity — it is never shared with other users or third parties.
  • All calls between finders and owners are routed through a masked relay number. Neither party's real number is ever revealed.
  • We do not sell, share, or monetize your personal data with any third parties.
  • You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the app.
  • SMS OTP messages are used solely for account verification and login authentication — never for marketing.
  • We comply with all applicable US telecommunications and privacy regulations.

How We Use SMS

STIQR uses SMS exclusively for one-time password (OTP) verification during account registration and login. We do not send promotional, marketing, or unsolicited messages of any kind.

Message frequency: One SMS per login or registration attempt, sent only when explicitly requested by the user.

Sample verification message:

Your STIQR verification code is 847291. Valid for 10 minutes. Do not share this code with anyone.

Opt-out: Reply STOP to unsubscribe from SMS messages at any time. Reply HELP for support.

Standard message and data rates may apply. STIQR uses Twilio toll-free SMS services for reliable, compliant message delivery across the United States and Canada.

ABOUT STIQR

STIQR (pronounced "sticker") is a mobile application available on Android and iOS. The app enables users to protect their valuables by attaching smart QR stickers that allow finders to contact owners anonymously through masked calling — powered by Twilio's toll-free number infrastructure.

The service is currently free to use. Our goal is to keep it free forever while maintaining the highest standards of user privacy and data security.
